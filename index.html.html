Environmental indicators

Your facility's discharges, energy use, and water consumption affect people who never set foot inside your building. Here's how.

Wastewater — BOD Biochemical Oxygen Demand
BOD measures how much organic pollution is in your wastewater. The higher it is, the more oxygen gets depleted in the water body it discharges into — harming aquatic life and making water unsafe.
Effluent BOD at discharge point
Measured from your effluent treatment plant (ETP) outlet
mg/L
Limit: ≤50 mg/L
Wastewater — COD Chemical Oxygen Demand
COD measures toxic organic and inorganic compounds in wastewater. High COD means your ETP is not removing enough pollutants before discharge — posing risks to anyone who uses that water downstream.
Effluent COD at discharge point
Measured from your effluent treatment plant (ETP) outlet
mg/L
Limit: ≤90 mg/L
